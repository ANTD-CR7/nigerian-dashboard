Key Insight - FX Unification

The June 2023 CBN foreign exchange unification reform caused the Naira to depreciate simultaneously against all major currencies. The NGN/USD rate moved from approximately NGN 461 to over NGN 1,600 within months. By April 2026 the Naira had partially stabilised at approximately NGN 1,361 per Dollar.
