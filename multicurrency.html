CBN buying, central and selling rates for 12 currencies - 2020-2026

Key Insight - FX Unification Reform

The June 2023 CBN foreign exchange unification reform caused the Naira to depreciate simultaneously against all major currencies. The NGN/USD central rate moved from approximately NGN 461 to over NGN 1,500 within months. By April 2026 the rate had partially stabilised near NGN 1,361/USD. All 12 CBN-quoted currencies experienced proportional depreciation during this period.
