Key Insight — 2020–2024

Nigeria's economy contracted in 2020 (COVID-19), recovered through 2021–2022, then faced surging inflation and a dramatic Naira depreciation following the June 2023 FX reform. The CBN responded with aggressive MPR hikes from 11.5% to 27.5% between 2022 and 2024.
