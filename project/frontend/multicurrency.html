Key Insight — FX Reform

The June 2023 CBN foreign exchange unification reform caused simultaneous Naira depreciation against all major currencies. NGN/USD moved from ₦461 to over ₦1,600 within months. By April 2026 the Naira had partially stabilised at approximately ₦1,361 per Dollar, reflecting improved oil revenues and CBN FX management reforms.
