Briefing Studio

Compose a cited, print-ready economic briefing from any indicators — generated live from the aggregated data

Compose your briefing

Choose up to 6 indicators (Ctrl/Cmd-click for several) and a window. Each section is built live: chart, statistics, event context and an APA citation — ready to print, save as PDF, or share as a link that regenerates it.

Nigeria — Economic Briefing

Methodology & honesty notes

Statistics are computed directly from the plotted observations: min/max with dates, mean, standard deviation, and an ordinary-least-squares trend with R² and a two-tailed significance p-value. Series are shown exactly as reported by their sources (no seasonal adjustment). Coverage differs per indicator and is stated in each section. Correlation and trend describe association, not causation. Full methods and limitations: the About page.
