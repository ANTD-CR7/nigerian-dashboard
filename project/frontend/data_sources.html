NPEDATA / Data Sources

Data Sources

Every figure on this platform traces back to one of three official public institutions. No estimates, no third-party resellers — primary sources only.

Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN)

Nigeria's central monetary authority. Publishes official exchange rates, the Monetary Policy Rate, foreign reserves, the NFEM daily market report, currency-in-circulation figures and its own balance sheet.

Data quality note: CBN figures are published as official administrative records, not survey estimates — the highest-confidence tier of data on this platform. Source: cbn.gov.ng

National Bureau of Statistics (NBS)

Nigeria's official statistics agency. Publishes the Consumer Price Index (inflation) and quarterly GDP reports, including the full 57-sector real GDP breakdown.

Data quality note: NBS Consumer Price Index methodology was rebased in 2024; this platform uses the headline year-on-year series as published, without retroactive adjustment. Source: nigerianstat.gov.ng

World Bank Open Data

International development institution. Provides Nigeria's nominal GDP in current US Dollars, used as a cross-check against the NBS/CBN domestic series.

Data quality note: USD-denominated GDP can fall even when local real GDP grows, since it is also a function of the Naira exchange rate — see the GDP page for the side-by-side comparison. Source: data.worldbank.org
