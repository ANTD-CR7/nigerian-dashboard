Key Insight - 2020-2026

Nigeria's gross foreign reserves fell from $38B in January 2020 to a low of $32.8B in December 2023, then recovered strongly to $49.7B by March 2026 driven by improved oil revenues and CBN FX policy reforms. The blocked reserves represent assets under lien or encumbrance that cannot be freely used.
