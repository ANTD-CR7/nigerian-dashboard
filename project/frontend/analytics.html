FX Reserves and the Exchange Rate — a weaker link than assumed

It's often assumed reserves and the exchange rate move inversely. Over 2020–2026 the data disagrees: as the Naira weakened, reserves dipped to a $32.5B low (April 2024) then recovered, so both actually trend upward. Both series are standardised (z-scores) to share one honest axis rather than a misleading dual axis.
