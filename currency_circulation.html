Currency in Circulation

Total Nigerian Naira in circulation - Central Bank of Nigeria - 2002-2024

Key Insight - Naira Redesign 2023

Currency in circulation dropped sharply in early 2023 following the CBN Naira redesign policy requiring old notes to be returned to banks. It recovered as courts challenged the policy. Long-term trend shows consistent growth from approximately ₦1.4T in 2002 to over ₦4T by 2024.
