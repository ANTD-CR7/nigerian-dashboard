Key Insight

Currency in circulation dropped sharply in early 2023 following the CBN Naira redesign policy requiring old notes to be returned to banks. It recovered as courts challenged the policy. Long-term trend shows consistent growth from ₦1.4T in 2002 to over ₦4T by 2024.
